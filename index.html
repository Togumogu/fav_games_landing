Wee, here I am explaining my favorite video games. I thought about it a lot and couldn't decide on any other subject matter. I guess classics don't die.
My All-Time Favorite Games!
God of War is one of my all-time favorites. The gameplay, the exploration, the puzzles a complete package.
What can be said about Red Dead Redemption 2 that is already not said. I'm not sure. The game is gorgeous and the story tragic.
Bloodborne is hard and has an excellent environment. It drives you mad, the bosses, the repetition, let the hunt begin.
The king of RPGs Fallout New Vegas! You can be anyone, kill anyone and well kill anyone? With excellent atmosphere find out if war ever changes?
Another classic, sad they don't make games like Dragon Age Origins anymore. A shame really, The map, the factions, the dept of rpg is a breath of freshness
It took me a while to get used to Dark Souls 3 but I finally grasped it. And then completed it thrice, including the DLCs. It's an addictions
Well, all of the games above are excellent masterpieces. They are the best of the best but more importantly my best. So my favorite game is: God of War
